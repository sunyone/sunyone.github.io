2. 监控zookpeer

  • key定义：
zookeeper.ruok.status[172.16.64.34,2181]
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_avg_latency] #响应一个客户端请求的时间，建议这个时间大于10个Tick就报警
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_max_latency] #响应一个客户端请求的时间，建议这个时间大于10个Tick就报警
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_min_latency] #响应一个客户端请求的时间，建议这个时间大于10个Tick就报警
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_packets_received]
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_packets_sent]
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_num_alive_connections]
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_outstanding_requests] #排队请求的数量，当ZooKeeper超过了它的处理能力时，这个值会增大，建议设置报警阀值为10
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_server_state]
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_znode_count] #znodes的数量
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_watch_count] #watches的数量
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_ephemerals_count]
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_approximate_data_size]
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_open_file_descriptor_count] #最大允许打开的文件数，由ulimit控制
zookeeper.mntr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_max_file_descriptor_count] #打开文件数量，当这个值大于允许值得85%时报警
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,version]
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Latency]
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Received] #接收到客户端请求的包数量
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Sent] #发送给客户端的包数量，主要是响应和通知
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Connections]
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Outstanding]
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Zxid]
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Mode] #运行的角色，如果没有加入集群就是standalone,加入集群式follower或者leader
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,Node]
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_followers] #leader角色才会有这个输出,集合中follower的个数。正常的值应该是集合成员的数量减1
zookeeper.srvr.status[172.16.64.34,2181,zk_pending_syncs] #leader角色才会有这个输出，pending syncs的数量

zookeeper.conf.status[127.0.0.1,2181,maxClientCnxns]
  • 参数：
vim zabbix_agentd.d/check_zookeeper.conf

UserParameter=zookeeper.ruok.status[*],echo ruok|nc $1 $2
UserParameter=zookeeper.mntr.status[*],echo mntr|nc $1 $2|grep $3|cut -f2
UserParameter=zookeeper.srvr.status[*],echo srvr|nc $1 $2|grep $3|awk -F': +' '{print $$2}'

4. 触发器

zookeeper排队请求数量过多

{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_outstanding_requests].count(#3,10,gt)}=3 and
{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_outstanding_requests].nodata(5m)}=0

zookeeper处理平均时延过高

{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_avg_latency].count(#3,10,gt)}=3 and
{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_avg_latency].nodata(5m)}=0

zookeeper连接数超过上限的85%

{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_num_alive_connections].last()}/{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.conf.status[127.0.0.1,2181,maxClientCnxns].last()}>0.85 and
{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_num_alive_connections].nodata(5m)}=0 and
{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.conf.status[127.0.0.1,2181,maxClientCnxns].nodata(5m)}=0

zookeeper文件打开数超过上限的85%

{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_open_file_descriptor_count].last()}/{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_max_file_descriptor_count].last()}>0.85 and
{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_open_file_descriptor_count].nodata(5m)}=0 and
{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.mntr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_max_file_descriptor_count].nodata(5m)}=0

zookeeper准备同步数过大

{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.srvr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_pending_syncs].count(#3,10,gt)}=3 and
{INM_Template_Zookeeper:zookeeper.srvr.status[127.0.0.1,2181,zk_pending_syncs].nodata(5m)}=0
